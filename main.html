about

Design, Code, and Innovation: Front-end Developer based in Incheon, South Korea.

I'm a frontend developer dedicated to crafting seamless, engaging, and visually compelling web experiences. My focus is on building intuitive and user-friendly interfaces that not only enhance usability but also create meaningful interactions. I believe that great design isn’t just about aesthetics—it’s about creating experiences that feel natural, effortless, and enjoyable. My background in fashion production has given me a unique perspective on design, precision, and project coordination. Understanding how visual elements harmonize allows me to bring a refined aesthetic to front-end development, ensuring interfaces are both functional and visually cohesive. Additionally, my experience working across teams has strengthened my ability to collaborate effectively with designers, developers, and stakeholders to bring ideas to life. I am deeply committed to writing clean, efficient code and continuously improving my skill set. I enjoy staying up to date with emerging technologies and best practices, always seeking innovative ways to enhance user engagement. Whether it’s refining micro-interactions, optimizing performance, or ensuring accessibility, I approach every project with creativity, attention to detail, and a problem-solving mindset. For me, frontend development is more than just writing code—it’s about shaping experiences, bridging design and technology, and bringing ideas to life in a way that truly connects with users.
